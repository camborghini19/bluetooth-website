A Scroll-Powered Guide to Wireless Connectivity

What is Bluetooth?

Bluetooth is a short-range wireless technology that connects devices using radio waves in the 2.4 GHz frequency band.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Discovery: Devices search using inquiry scanning.
  2. Pairing: Secure authentication is established.
  3. Piconet: One master connects with up to 7 slaves.
  4. Data Transfer: FHSS rapidly switches channels.
  5. BLE: Bluetooth Low Energy reduces power usage.

Key Concepts

  • Piconet: Mini-network with one master.
  • Scatternet: Multiple piconets interconnected.
  • FHSS: Frequency-hopping to avoid interference.
  • BLE: Designed for ultra-low power usage.
